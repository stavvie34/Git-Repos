Our Vineyard

Develop and operate a world-class winegrowing Vineyard. To produce organically grown blueberries and with this berry make a singular wine of the highest caliber and distinction. The style of the wine should be at once classic and contemporary... it should express the uniqueness of the precious resource that is Azure Vineyard.

Araujo Estate is located in the northeast Portland, just east of Salem. The estate was established around the Azure Vineyard, first planted over 7 years ago. Since the inaugural release bearing the Azure name in 2009, this unique vineyard has been widely recognized as one of the greatest terroirs in the Portland, Oregon.
