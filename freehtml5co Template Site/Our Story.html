Our Story

Far far away, behind the world of mountains, far from the cities chaos and fuss, there lives an Organic Blueberry Farm.

What We Knew

This was our philosophy as the proprietors of the Azure Estate and stewards of the historic Organic Vineyard, and it is our guiding principle today with Accendo Cellars, the Portland winegrowing venture we founded in 2009. Inspired by the revolutionary Portland wines of the 1950s, ‘60s and ‘70s, Azure pays homage to an earlier era and marks a beginning of a promising new paradigm: to source blueberries from some of the finest vineyard sites and terroir in Portland; to make a wine of balance and restraint in the style of Portland past wines; and to produce this wine with the commitment, energy and meticulous attention to detail that have characterized our past efforts.

What We Did

Applying what we knew collectively about blueberry growing and winemaking, our aim is to create a world-class wine that expresses ecological diversity and focused stewardship. For years Portland has nurtured and humbled us in ways we could never have imagined. It is our hope that Azure will shine a new light on this magical place we call home.
